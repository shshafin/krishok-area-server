Web Development Proposal

EQUAÇÕES RAZOÁVEIS EMPRESA DE TRABALHO TEMPORÁRIO LDA

Marceline Studios is pleased to submit this proposal for the design and development of a comprehensive recruitment portal. Our solution focuses on a robust content management system, multi-language support (Portuguese/English), and a seamless user experience for both employers and job seekers.

1. Project Objectives

  • Digital Presence: Establish a professional online identity for EQUAÇÕES RAZOÁVEIS.
  • Process Automation: Streamline the connection between employers requesting workers and candidates finding jobs.
  • Accessibility: Ensure the platform is accessible in Portuguese (default) with translation options.
  • Lead Generation: Integrate scheduling tools for advisory meetings.

2. Core Features

Frontend Functionality
  • Advanced Job Search Engine
  • Advisory Meeting Scheduler
  • Portuguese Language (Default)
  • Google Translator Integration
Backend Power
  • Dynamic Content Management
  • Role-Based Access Control
  • Fast Server-Side Rendering
  • Secure Data Handling

Technical Specification

4. Administration System

The platform will include a secure Admin Panel with a strict hierarchy to ensure data integrity and management control.

Super Admin

The highest level of authority. Has full access to all settings, job postings, and content. Exclusive Capability: Can create, manage, and delete other 'Admin' accounts.

Admin

Operational level access. Can manage job listings, review applications, update blog posts, and handle day-to-day site content. Restriction: Cannot create or delete other admin users.
